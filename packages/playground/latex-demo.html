Completions

  • Type \ for macro completions (e.g. \term, \section).
  • Type \begin{ for environment snippets — they expand to a full \begin…\end skeleton.
  • Inside $…$, completions switch to KaTeX math macros (try \fra → \frac).
  • \ref{ / \cref{ complete against \label{}s in the document.
  • Trigger manually with Ctrl+Space.

Validation

  • Unknown environments are flagged (see bogusenv).
  • Unclosed or mismatched \begin/\end pairs are errors.
  • Unrecognized macros get a subtle hint; \newcommand definitions are respected.
  • Comments and verbatim blocks are never flagged.

Notes
